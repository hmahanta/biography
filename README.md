## 👤 About Me

I'm an Enterprise Architect and AI Platform Designer with **nearly 19 years** of experience designing and modernizing enterprise financial and ERP systems - spanning PeopleSoft FSCM, PeopleSoft HCM, Campus Solutions, and Oracle Fusion. Over the last several years, my focus has shifted toward a specific problem: **how do you bring metadata-driven architecture and explainable AI into enterprise financial systems without sacrificing governance, auditability, or financial accuracy?**

That question is the throughline across everything on this profile from **ERPKit**, an open-source Python standard library for enterprise transaction validation, to **AuditIQ AI** and **ARIP**, AI platforms designed to sit above existing ERP systems rather than replace them.

I'd rather build a reusable framework once than rebuild the same integration logic on every project - which is why most of my recent work lives in the open.

## 🧠 Enterprise AI Platforms

### 🛡️ AuditIQ AI
**Enterprise AI Platform for Compliance Intelligence**

AuditIQ AI is designed to close a specific gap in enterprise compliance tooling: most compliance systems tell you *that* something is wrong, not *why* it matters, *which* exceptions to act on first, or *what* to do about it. AuditIQ AI is built to answer all three.

<div align="center">

| Traditional Compliance Tooling | AuditIQ AI |
|---|---|
| Flags an error | Explains **WHY** it occurred, using regulatory context |
| Produces a flat exception list | Prioritizes **WHICH** exceptions matter most, by risk and materiality |
| Stops at detection | Recommends **WHAT** corrective action to take next |

</div>

**Capabilities**
- Cross-border VAT / GST Compliance
- Statutory Reconciliation
- Audit Readiness Assessment
- Semantic Compliance Intelligence
- Explainable AI for Regulatory Decisions
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation over Regulatory Knowledge Graphs
- Compliance Recommendation Engine
- Exception Prioritization
- Corrective Action Guidance

### 📈 ARIP — Autonomous Receivables Intelligence Platform

ARIP is an AI-native **Financial Intelligence Layer** that sits above existing ERP systems - Oracle Fusion, SAP, PeopleSoft, Workday, Microsoft Dynamics — rather than replacing them. It's built around a simple distinction:

<div align="center">

**Your ERP tells you what happened. ARIP predicts what will happen next.**

</div>

**Capabilities**
- Payment Delay Prediction
- Default Risk Scoring
- Working Capital Optimization
- DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) Reduction
- Cash Forecasting
- AI Recommendation Engine
- Finance Copilot
- Executive Dashboards
- Predictive Collections
- Customer Payment Behaviour Analytics
